 dev-vcs/git-lfs, Gentoo Mono Handbook
 
https://help.github.com/articles/billing-plans-for-git-large-file-storage/

Personal and organization accounts using Git LFS receive 1 GB total for storage.
Additional storage and bandwidth is offered as a single pack.
One pack costs $5 per month, and provides a monthly quota of 50 GB for bandwidth and 50 GB for storage.
You can purchase as many packs as you need.
For example, if you need 150 GB of storage, you'd buy three packs.
